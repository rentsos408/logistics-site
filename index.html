Who We Are

RENTSOS408 is a global consulting firm specializing in relocation, sourcing, procurement, and logistics solutions for individuals and businesses. We assist clients in securing and consolidating goods, arranging shipments, and navigating international trade logistics. Our expertise spans medical equipment, household goods, and personal items, ensuring tailored solutions for diverse needs.

Beyond logistics, we also help clients during their relocation, sourcing furniture for their new homes, offering assistance with opening bank accounts, securing temporary housing, décor, and essential household items to create a comfortable living space. Our services are particularly valuable for retirees looking to transition smoothly to a new country.

As a registered Maersk account holder, we offer direct shipping coordination without relying on traditional freight forwarders, allowing for cost-effective and transparent logistics. We work with trusted suppliers and logistics partners worldwide to provide seamless, end-to-end support from procurement to final delivery.

With services available in English, Spanish, Italian, and French, RENTOS408 helps clients streamline their supply chain while ensuring efficiency, competitive pricing, and personalized guidance throughout the process.
